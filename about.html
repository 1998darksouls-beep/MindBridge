About MindBridge

Bridging the gap between mental health awareness and professional support in Pakistan.

The Challenge

1 in 4 people affected globally by mental health issues at some point in their lives.

Severe Shortage: Pakistan faces an extreme deficit of licensed psychologists per capita.

Social Stigma: Cultural barriers block transparent help-seeking behaviors.

High Barriers: Geographic distance and high entry costs prevent early intervention.

Our Approach

We utilize internationally validated clinical metrics to provide accurate insights:

  • ✓ PHQ-9 for Depression tracking.
  • ✓ GAD-7 for Anxiety assessment.
  • ✓ PSS-10 for Perceived Stress.

Ethical Commitment

MindBridge operates on strict ethical guidelines:

  • Data Anonymity: All score calculations happen locally on your device. We do not store or transmit your answers.
  • No Server Storage: Your privacy is absolute. There is no backend database saving your profile.
  • Crisis Visibility: Helplines are perpetually visible and prioritized for at-risk users.
  • AI Boundaries: Our AI chatbot provides psychoeducation and coping mechanisms. It does not replace human care or therapy.
